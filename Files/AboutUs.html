Our mission at Travelicious



Our mission is to inspire, inform, and equip Indians to have European trips that are fun, affordable, and culturally broadening. We value travel as a powerful way to better understand and contribute to the world in which we live. We strive to keep our own travel style, our world outlook, and our business practices consistent with these values.

Why Choose Us?

Find a Different Story

You're tired of the same old story; bored by routine. At Travelicious, we specialise in curating imaginative, immersive journeys that are anything but predictable.

Travel with Confidence

You're unique; so, shouldn't your holiday be, too? At Travelicious, we don't sell 'off the peg'. Instead, we'll work with you to design a bespoke itinerary that suits you and your values, right down to the ground.

Maki it Count

Your time is precious, but so's our planet. You want to travel mindfully - to contribute, not simply consume - but where to begin? At Travelicious, sustainable travel has always been way more than a mere trend.
